Platform

Share is a totally transparent, direct-democratic skill-sharing platform where you can help your community, build your reputation and learn some brand new skills! If you've ever wanted legal advice but not wanted to pay for it, needed therapy but been unable to get an appointment, or just wanted some help with your university project or work presentation, Share is the solution for you! Our win-win-win model promises to return value to Volunteers, Users and Sponsors by transforming work into social capital and creating a Wikipedia-like challenge to the standard economy of exchange. Built on a commitment to the idea that social exchange has real material value, Share is a tool to empower your community, boost your profile and create some real value in the world.

Volunteers

Volunteers are an integral part of the platform as they are the ones volunteering their skills and time to the community. As a volunteer you can:

  • Offer your skills and services pro gratis to your local community or internationally.
  • Choose when, where, and how much time to volunteer.
  • Get good feels from helping out, which is scientifically guaranteed to improve the world and how you feel about it.

Users

Users are the main beneficiaries of the platform as they are offered a wide range of free support, knowledge and skills. As a user you can:

  • Learn new things for free, connecting you to a wide community.
  • Participate in a totally transparent system, leaving ratings and reviews for volunteers.
  • Feel more secure knowing that community members are there to help each other.
